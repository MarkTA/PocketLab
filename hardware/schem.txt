Wiring

AD9833 pin     NodeMCU-32S pin
SCLK    <--->  GPIO 18
SDATA   <--->  GPIO 23
FSYNC   <--->  GPIO 5
DGND    <--->  GND
VCC     <--->  3V3

Final X9C wiring:

VL → buffered VREF
VH → conditioned AD9833 signal after the 47 µF capacitor
VW → OP484 pin 3
VCC → 4.75 V
GND → common ground
CS → ESP32 GPIO25
INC → ESP32 GPIO26 through 1 kΩ
U/D → ESP32 GPIO27
